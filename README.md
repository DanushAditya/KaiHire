# 🚀 Kai Placement Copilot

Kai Placement Copilot is an **AI-powered placement preparation platform** designed to help students systematically prepare for job placements through structured learning, daily challenges, progress tracking, and resume insights.

The platform acts as a **personal placement mentor**, guiding students with personalized plans, coding/aptitude challenges, streak tracking, and leaderboard-based motivation.

---

## 🎯 Problem

Many students struggle with placement preparation because:

* There is **no structured preparation plan**
* Students lose consistency while practicing
* Progress tracking is unclear
* Resume insights and improvement suggestions are limited
* Preparation often feels **isolated and unmotivating**

Kai Placement Copilot solves this by creating a **gamified and AI-assisted preparation ecosystem.**

---

## 💡 Solution

Kai Placement Copilot provides a **centralized preparation platform** where students can:

* Upload their **resume for analysis**
* Get a **personalized preparation plan**
* Solve **daily coding and aptitude challenges**
* Maintain **preparation streaks**
* Compete through a **leaderboard system**
* Track learning progress over time

The system combines **AI assistance, gamification, and structured learning** to keep students consistent and motivated.
